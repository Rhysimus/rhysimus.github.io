Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Rhys, a first year degree apprentice, avid technology lover, boba drinker and coding enthusiast.

Why am I running for Secretary?

Being on committee has given me insight into how wonderful CSS is and how everything works. I want to take this further by taking on the role of Secretary - who supports the committee, organises and minutes the meetings and keeps up to date with communications.

And what do I have for the role?

I've been on the current committee as First Year Representative, which has taught me a lot about CSS and how to make it awesome for you. The society of course, not the language. The language scares me. Outside of that, I'm someone who always listens and is able to manage my time effectively. I've also had the pleasure to recently undertake the roles of Secretary, which I thank the current committee that gave me the chance to learn.

How will I look out for the members?

As someone who always wants to look out for others and appeal to what they want, I'd love to be able to take suggestions and listen to what you have to say - you're the number one voice! I also want to embrace the warm nature of the society, and continue the legacy of awesome events and member engagement for you within the committee.

What are my three key points?

  1. To continue engagement between the members and committee
  2. To provide encouragement and momentum to spur the flow of events from the society
  3. To ensure that support is readily available to everyone

Any questions? Please let me know!

I hope to see you at the AGM! Best of luck everyone!
